Manudama


'Manudama', is a series of kuppi sessions conducted by the HRM to assist our fellow students in their academic arena. A few subjects of the curriculum due for each academic year are chosen and kuppi sessions are conducted by senior students who have produced excellent results in the respective subjects. A comprehensive note on the particular subject is also provided in each of these kuppi sessions. As the kuppi sessions are scheduled beforehand and properly notified to students,a large number of students turn up for every kuppi session resulting in the success of Manudama.
